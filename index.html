Description

USP

Overcome your nightmares to free yourself from an endless slumber in the Dreamcore, horror game Anemoia.

Detail

Anemoia will be an extremely unique style of horror game, featuring a weirdcore aesthetic and a series of challenging puzzles that test your knowledge of space, perspective, and your trust in the inhabitants of Anemoia’s strange worlds. The game will take place inside of the player character’s (PC’s) dreams, and will feature places, objects, people, and concepts that should feel familiar to the player in some ways, yet alien in others. While inside a dream, our brain tricks us into believing in whatever it is we see and interact with. In Anemoia however, the player will be driven to question their surroundings at every turn, as they seamlessly transition from a pleasant, nostalgia-inducing dream to a dark, otherworldly nightmare.
