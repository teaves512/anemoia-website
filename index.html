The Game

Anemoia will release this year, 2022, on October 1st!

Wishlist now, in time for Halloween!

Wishlist on Steam Wishlist on GOG

Anemoia is an extremely unique horror game, featuring a dreamcore aesthetic and a series of challenging puzzles that test your knowledge of space, perspective, and trust in the inhabitants of Anemoia's strange worlds. The game takes place inside of your dreams, and contains places, objects, people and concepts that should feel familiar to you but will seem confusingly out of place. You will be driven to question your surroundings and the advice of the inhabitants of your subconcious mind.

Your story begins as you fall asleep, and find yourself in a pleasant, nostalgia-inducing dream. However, these feelings of comfort will not last long as you seamlessly transition into a dark, otherworldly nightmare. Here, you will be challenged by the beings of your subconcious mind, that represent different parts of your personality. Be careful who to trust, and resist becoming consumed by the evil these beings portray.

Description

USP

Overcome your nightmares to free yourself from an endless slumber in the Dreamcore, horror game Anemoia.

Detail

Anemoia will be an extremely unique style of horror game, featuring a weirdcore aesthetic and a series of challenging puzzles that test your knowledge of space, perspective, and your trust in the inhabitants of Anemoia’s strange worlds. The game will take place inside of the player character’s (PC’s) dreams, and will feature places, objects, people, and concepts that should feel familiar to the player in some ways, yet alien in others. While inside a dream, our brain tricks us into believing in whatever it is we see and interact with. In Anemoia however, the player will be driven to question their surroundings at every turn, as they seamlessly transition from a pleasant, nostalgia-inducing dream to a dark, otherworldly nightmare.
